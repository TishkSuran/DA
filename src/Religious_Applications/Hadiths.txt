Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "The merciful are shown mercy by the Most Merciful. Show mercy to those on earth, and He Who is in the heaven will show mercy to you." (Sunan Abi Dawud)
The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "Truthfulness leads to righteousness, and righteousness leads to Paradise. And a man keeps on telling the truth until he becomes a truthful person. Falsehood leads to Al-Fajur (i.e., wickedness, evil-doing), and Al-Fajur (wickedness) leads to the (Hell) Fire, and a man may keep on telling lies till he is written before Allah, a liar." (Sahih al-Bukhari)
Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "He who has in his heart the weight of a mustard seed of pride shall not enter Paradise." (Sahih Muslim)
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Paradise lies at the feet of your mother." (Musnad Ahmad)
The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: "The honest and trustworthy merchant will be with the prophets, the truthful, and the martyrs on the Day of Judgment." (Jami` at-Tirmidhi)
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "The strong man is not the one who can overpower others (in wrestling); rather, the strong man is the one who controls himself when he gets angry." (Sahih al-Bukhari)
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "The believer's shade on the Day of Resurrection will be his charity." (Al-Tirmidhi)
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day should maintain good relations with his kindred." (Sahih al-Bukhari)
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "The best of you are those who are the best to their wives." (Sunan Ibn Majah)
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Seeking knowledge is obligatory upon every Muslim." (Sunan Ibn Majah)
